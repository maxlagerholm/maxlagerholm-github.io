Passion for people and creativity

Coming from a broad background of many different fields and experiences, the title sums it up very well who I am. Before pursuing a career in UX Design I've worked as a sushi chef, special needs teaching assistant, event builder, English teacher and photographer. I also went on a four year long journey where I spent two years in Australia on a Working Holiday Visa and additional two years of backpacking around South East Asia. I traveled with an open mind, often to smaller areas where I met and stayed with locals. Due to Covid19 I finally returned back home in August 2020 and since August 2021 I'm studying to become a UX Designer where I can combine my intrests of human interactions and creativity.
