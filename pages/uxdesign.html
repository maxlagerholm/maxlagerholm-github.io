PiXER. The datingapp where you match without a picture.

A project where the assignment was to create a dating app where pictures wasn't the main focus when matching with others. The idea of my platform was to find people based on "tags" and criteria, read the suggested profiles, and start a conversation. Once in a conversation you would unlock pictures as you interact with each other.

PiXER

Rädda Maten. Don't waste your food, give it to someone else!

A group project where the assignment was to find a big problem in our everyday life and make up a solution for it. We created an application to help reduce food waste. A networking app for dorms and school campuses to share food that one might not need or want instead of throwing it away.

Rädda Maten

Espresso House. Order your daily coffee on the go.

A project I made as part of applying for my UX Design education. The assignment was to improve an already existing product. My choice was to improve the design and user experience of Sweden's biggest coffee shop franchise (similar to Starbucks).
